About Us




TEAI - TOE's Earth and Space Initiative

TEAI is the Science and Technology Initiative of TOE with the aim of protecting the Earthâ€™s Environment and reaching for the horizons by spreading the wings of knowledge and making aware every individual out there about Earth and Space. The motto behind this Initiative of TOE is introducing the youth, to the emerging and futuristic fields, which is a combination of Earth and Space. TEAI conducts various Earth and Space Awareness programs in schools and introduces the students to things like outer space, how satellites work, and monitoring climate change.


TFF - The Future Forward

The Future Forward is an International Summit being organized to expose the youth and the participants to modern topics covering Future trends of Earth and Space. The summit aims to raise awareness and educate the participants by intuitive discussion and workshops led by experts of the different futuristic fields.



Expected Outcomes of the Summit



Research


We expect to lay the groundwork for a strong foundation to help facilitate a sustainable way of living through small and big solutions to the issues discussed in the summit.

Education


The summit covers an array of modern topics in the fields of Environment, Earth, and Space. We believe that educating the youth and raising awareness is essential to achieve a sustainable way of living.

Collaboration

TEAI through the TFF summit 2021 strives to collaborate with environmental organizations and technological industry giants to come up with creative innovations in an effort to ensure the survival of species of the Earth in their future.
